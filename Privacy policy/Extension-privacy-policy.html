Epic Games Library Exporter does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All processing happens locally on your computer, and your information never leaves your device.

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how the Epic Games Library Exporter Chrome extension ("the Extension") handles user data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. The Extension:

  • Does NOT collect personal information
  • Does NOT track your browsing activity
  • Does NOT store your Epic Games account information
  • Does NOT send data to any servers
  • Does NOT use analytics or tracking tools
  • Does NOT share data with third parties

How the Extension Works

The Extension operates entirely within your browser:

  • Local Processing: When you export your library, the Extension reads data from the Epic Games website and processes it locally on your computer
  • Direct Download: The processed data is saved directly to your computer as a text file
  • No Server Communication: At no point does the Extension communicate with external servers
  • User-Initiated: All actions are initiated by you - nothing happens automatically

Data Storage

The Extension uses Chrome's local storage API only to save your export preferences (such as which fields to include in the export). This data:

  • Is stored locally on your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Can be cleared by removing the Extension
  • Contains only your preference settings, not personal data

Permissions Explained

The Extension requires certain permissions to function. Here's why we need each one:

Permission Why It's Needed
scripting To inject the export functionality into the Epic Games account page
storage To save your export preferences locally
activeTab To interact with the current tab when you click the Extension
tabs To verify you're on the correct Epic Games page and open help pages
epicgames.com/account/* Limited access only to Epic Games account pages for data export

Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use any third-party services or analytics tools. It operates independently without any external dependencies.

Data Security

Since we don't collect or transmit any data, there are no security risks associated with data breaches or unauthorized access to our servers. Your Epic Games data is processed locally and saved directly to your computer.

Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. Since we don't collect any data at all, this protection is inherently provided.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Open Source

The Extension is open source. You can review the entire source code on our GitHub repository to verify our privacy practices.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You maintain complete control over your exported data files, which are stored locally on your computer.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the Extension's data practices, please:
